How to Delete Your Account in Connecto Driver

To delete your account:

  1. Open the Connecto Driver app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Press the Delete Account Button
  4. Confirm deletion.

Data deleted:

  • Account info (email, profile, etc.)
  • App activity and preferences

Data retained (if any):

  • Anonymous crash logs (without personal identity)
